Our Story

Forging Champions Since 2009

FORGE was born from a simple belief: that every person has untapped potential waiting to be unleashed. What started as a small warehouse gym has grown into a premier fitness destination trusted by over 5,000 members.

Our founders, former competitive athletes, understood that true transformation requires more than just equipment — it demands expert coaching, a supportive community, and an environment that pushes you beyond your comfort zone.

Our Journey

Milestones That Defined Us

From a humble beginning to becoming the city's most trusted fitness brand — here's how FORGE was built.

2009
The First Hammer Strike
Founded in a 2,000 sq ft warehouse with second-hand equipment and a dream to change lives through fitness.
2013
1,000 Members Strong
Expanded to a 10,000 sq ft facility and reached our first thousand members, proving our approach works.
2017
Award-Winning Facility

2024
5,000 Members & Growing
Continuing to expand our programs, technology, and community impact. The forge never stops burning.
