Our Story

Founded in 2010, Coffee & Co. began as a small neighborhood cafe with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. Our founders, Sarah and Michael, believed that great coffee could bring people together and create meaningful connections.

Today, with locations across the city, we remain committed to our original values. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms, roast them to perfection in-house, and train our baristas to craft each cup with care and precision.

But we're more than just a coffee shop. We're a community hub where friends meet, ideas are born, and everyone feels at home. Whether you're stopping by for your morning espresso or settling in for an afternoon of work, we're here to make your day better, one cup at a time.
